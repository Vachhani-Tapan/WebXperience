Adaptive Audio

Automatically adjusts the listening experience based on environmental conditions and fit. The H2 chip analyzes external noise 48,000 times per second to optimize sound quality.

  • Real-time environmental noise analysis
  • Dynamic EQ adjustment based on ear seal
  • Personalized sound profile using machine learning
  • Automatic switching between ANC and Transparency
  • Computational audio processing

Personalized Spatial Audio with Dynamic Head Tracking

Immersive 3D sound that follows your head movements for a theater-like experience. Using built-in gyroscopes and accelerometers, AirPods Pro track the motion of your head and adjust the sound field accordingly.

  • Dynamic head tracking with motion sensors
  • 3D audio positioning in real-time
  • Dolby Atmos and spatial audio support
  • Personalized HRTF (Head-Related Transfer Function)
  • Works with Apple Music, Apple TV+, and more
  • Compatible with iPhone, iPad, and Mac
